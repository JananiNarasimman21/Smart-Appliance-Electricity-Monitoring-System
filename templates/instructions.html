🔌 Smart Plug Setup Instructions

Follow the steps below to connect your appliance using a smart plug and monitor electricity consumption using this dashboard.

🔌

Step 1

Insert the smart plug into a wall power socket.

⚡

Step 2

Connect your appliance (AC, geyser, microwave, etc.) to the smart plug.

📶

Step 3

Connect the smart plug to your home WiFi using its mobile application.

📱

Step 4

Ensure your monitoring system and smart plug are on the same network.

📊

Step 5

Open the energy monitoring dashboard on this website.

📈

Step 6

Select an appliance to view daily, monthly, or real-time electricity usage.


Note: These steps apply to most smart plugs. The setup process may vary slightly depending on the brand.
